The Smart Set Illustrations

Before its discontinuation in 2024, The Smart Set was an online magazine covering pop culture, arts, science, and current affairs. Through a Pennoni Honors College co-op, weekly article illustrations were created, including a feature and an accompanying image for each narrative. The digital format offered an opportunity to experiment with diverse art styles for online publication.
