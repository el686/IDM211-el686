2024 Star Scholars Summer Showcase

The STAR Scholars Summer Showcase celebrates the research and creative work of Drexel’s STAR Scholars in the Undergraduate Research and Enrichment Program. This booklet design incorporates the program’s type logo to represent each student’s unique “star” value in their research. Used during the final week of the Summer Term, the design also supported the event with wayfinding and presenter name tags.
